Address Types

Unicast Addresses
- Single IP Address (Source IP address or Destination IP)
- A to B
- One to One 
- They must be unique within the same subnet (logical grouping of addresses)
	+ no duplicates within the same subnet: no duplicates 192.168.1.100
	+ TCP 

Broadcast Addresses
- Single IP Address, it's special--it's not assigned to any one device.
- Every device within a subnet listens for traffic sent to the broadcast address.
- One to All
- UDP
- ARP, DHCP

Multicast Addresses
- Neither Unicast or Broadcast
- subset of a broadcast
- one to many
